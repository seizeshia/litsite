About Us

We are a few friends that just love comics!

We needed More

Since we have been comic fans for sometime, we wanted a resource that could provide us with all the information that we needed at our fingertips. A few of us are developers by day, so we thought we may put something together to server our fellow comic fans. We started this project recently as a more of a hobby, but we would love for you and other comic fans to try our app out and tell us what you think. The project is still in its infancy but we plan on making updates and strive to make LitNerd the resource we have all been waiting for!

Our Goals

A rough timeline on what we currently have planned

  • 2017 June

    Launch a Simple Site

    Get this site up and a find a name that resonates with us nerds!

  • September 2017

    Launch Our App

    We believe mobile is the platform that would really solve a lot of the issues us comic fans have. We launch a very simple rough app that we plan on improving on.

  • March 2018

    Integrate Web and Mobile

    We want to integrate both our mobile and web resources into one that work in tandem. We want our users to be able to access the resource at home and on the go!

  • July 2018

    Iterate

    Iterate and make our products better, and hopefully make the best resource for comics!
